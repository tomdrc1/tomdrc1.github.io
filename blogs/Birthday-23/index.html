Birthday 23

It's that time of the year again, it's my birthday! I'm turning 23 now.

Before writing this I went back and read the last year note that I wrote (Birthday 22, makes sense, right?)

I'll start with the easy, I did learn 5 songs on the piano, that is if you consider learning chords as songs. I learned to play most of the chords and sometimes I sit down and record myself singing and playing. As for proper classical songs, I learned only 1, that's right, a full year and I managed to master only 1 song. To be fair I did spend a lot of time learning the chords. Only 2 months ago I actually got a proper teacher.

As for Australia, I did not visit it. I did go to South Korea, that was fun! And that little group I talked about that I went bouldering with? They are now some of my closest friends, we even do some dinners here and there.

Also, last year I said that I might find myself in another country, I didn't. I did however find a new job that is based in Spain, but has an office in SG. I'm actually super excited about starting there, my new boss seems like he cares, he took the time to meet me over dinner when he was in SG for 3 days. And the entire company is giving me good vibes. I hope I won't regret these words that I am typing.

Ok, as for the actual year, this one went by way quicker than the last one. The last one felt almost infinite. I'm not sure why really, maybe it's the routine? Maybe it's the fact that in SG there are no seasons except hot and humid? I can never know.

I don't really know what to write for this year, it was a bit more bleak, I honestly think I experienced full on depression for a while, that was not fun. But I got out of it, I think.

As for travel wise, this year was packed and not at the same time. At September, I went to Vietnam for a long weekend, then I went to Korea and Taiwan for a month, that was fun. I even did Snowboarding with the said friends from before! Then I was stale for a while. I didn't really feel like doing much. I went back home in April (I know, I didn't write about it, I didn't really feel like writing), And in July I went to a different country every weekend (A weekend in Bangkok to visit a good friend of mine, a weekend in Taiwan with another good friend and then Israel for a week). Actually, when I look back at it, I traveled quite a bit.

I landed back in SG today, on my birthday! I think the best thing that happened to me is that when I went to scan my passport to enter the country, the machine had a little cute "Happy Birthday!" animation. I was pleasantly surprised!

And actually today was quite nice although I was a bit bummed - to be jet legged during my birthday, but the girls in the pottery studio I go to invited me over and baked me a cake! That was super nice, and super awkward. I had like 20 people wishing me happy birthday. I did however go to 3 good friends to eat Korean army stew and watch a movie with them. That was quite fun. I did fall asleep during the movie though. I was jet lagged!

I guess that's it. It feels like a lot of stuff happened this year. I saw a lot of places, met a bunch of new people and did some job hunting. I just hope that everything will be a bit better next year. Although this year wasn't bad per say, but everything did feel really meaningless at times.

Also Maybe another small bucket list? I won't be as greedy, I want to learn 2 new classical pieces on the piano. And travel Europe, should be doable with my new job!
