Safe Travels!

When I was traveling, as you do, you meet quite a lot of people. Some I loved, some I hated and some are just neutral.

Usually when you say goodbye, both parties understand that we will probably never see each other again. So when you say your farewells you choose your words carefully, at least I tried to.

When I first had to say goodbye to one of the people I traveled with, he said something that stuck with me. "It was fun, hope everything will be good, and safe travels!".

Safe travels stuck with me. In a way, this backpacking trip is a journey, and what is the best wish I can give someone on a journey? To travel safe. I loved that, and ever since then, I used that when I said my goodbyes "I only wish you that everything will be good, and safe travels!".

I used to work in a university back in Israel, and right before we went into lockdown our professor told us that we will probably not see each other for a long time so we started wishing each other wishes. As we were talking and wishing stuff to each other, he said something that also stuck with me throughout the years: "Tom, as you grow older you realize that the most boring, and least exciting wishes are the ones we want."

"If I wish someone happiness, I truly mean it, I want him to only have happiness! Be healthy! No diseases or anything bad. All those other sophisticated wishes just don't make alot of sense when you think about it".

And I think safe travels fits right in. What do I want to wish to that person I'm parting my ways with? That he will reach all of his destinations safe and sound.
